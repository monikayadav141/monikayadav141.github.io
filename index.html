Monika Yadav

PhD Student in Sociology at Columbia University

I am a sociologist of gender, race, and politics. My research examines the social construction of gender and race in the political sphere, with a focus on the United States. I use a variety of methods, including qualitative interviews, archival research, and computational text analysis.

My dissertation, tentatively titled "The Gender of Politics," examines how gender shapes the political careers of women and men in the U.S. Congress. I am also working on a second project on the role of race in the 2020 presidential election.
